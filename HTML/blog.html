How technology affects daily life and business?

Twentieth first century, a century full of innovations and scientific crafts, changed our lifestyles with comfort and luxury. So, technology is simply the use of scientific knowledge for the welfare of all. To meet a human need or solve a human problem technology has become an integral part of industry as well as livelihood. Even a small pin of a secure alphanumeric or numeric code used for authenticated access to a system or a big jetliner used for great purposes are all the products of technology. Tools that are initial are developed by human beings which was a stone spear tied to a stick. Altogether technology affects daily life and business in positive or negative ways
Besides comforting our life in a luxurious way there are several positive impacts of technology in our daily life as well as in business. Some the major positive impacts that technology holds are listed below:

Positive Impacts:

1.Advanced Business and Marketing

With the ongoing modernization we are mostly seen spending more time on electronics like cell phones, tablets and laptops. Growing influence on social media creates a platform for small business creators to fluster their business and reach to their desired customers through all these devices by creating campaigns across social media, display advertising and e-commerce. It also enables them to interact, engage with their customers via popular technologies such as online messaging and digital advertising. Following technology can help certain marketing teams of any business by increasing efficiency, improve customer relationships.

2. Advanced Travelling and Transportation Facility

Growth in advancement of technology is also seen in the transportation field. Before we needed to go to the required office for booking tickets. Now it’s been more convenient that we can purchase tickets via our devices using websites like SeatGuru.

3. Distance Education and Modern Learning

Technology saved us from many problems that we suffered on a regular basis. After the breakdown of covid-19 ,lockdown was implemented everywhere , people were maintaining social distancing but e-learning platforms like zoom, google classroom etc helped us to stay connected and continue our education without any issues.

4. Secured Bill Payment

Technology really made our life a piece of heaven to live in. From waking up to the end of the day, we relied on machinery to get our work done. By incorporating digital wallets by locals like mobile wallets, QR payments made transactions more convenient and safer and maintained the relationship between customers and the dealers.

5. Easy Accessibility to Information

From any corner in the world just with one click on the internet we can get answers to any query. Platform like Google is a saviour for everyone. From study materials to solving medical issue problems, technological sites really helped us.

Negative Impacts

Everything that has a positive impact comes with some negativity too. Technology really upholds the most part of our lives but makes us more dependent on them. There are a lot of negative impacts of technology. With the advancement in use of digital platforms there is a high risk that our privacy is hampered.Nowadays we can hear different news about how people's accounts being hacked.Technology made our life comfortable and it's easy to find the solution of each and every problem with one click on internet which make us more dependent and mentally dull.one of the major issues that people are being victim of is online fraud. Several fake sites are being promoted on social media accusing large groups of people of money or stealing their personal information.Nowadays we can see that without any devices on hand it's impossible to feed kids . Sudden outbreak of the pandemic compelled us to stay in our home for a long time which made kids more focused on their electronics rather than studies. Mobile games like pubg, ML, free fire etc are diverting their mind from any sorts of recreational activities whereas they are having less quality time with family members.People are attracted towards online shopping which saves their time somehow but local stores present there are on the verge of closing.
Even in the photography area too, technology contributed a lot. Digital camera stands among other cameras in many ways. It is cost effective ,is a video camera,editing can be done easily without any issue,performs multiple operations, whereas the photos can be shared easily.Lastly, technology plays a huge importance in our day-to-day life. From the start of the day to the end of the day somehow, we are dependent on technology. We should learn to use technology in the proper manner so that it won't impact our life negatively. If we use it in a proper way then obviously it would bless us with enormous comfort and luxury.
